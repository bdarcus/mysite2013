I'm Bruce D'Arcus, an Associate Professor of Geography, and Chair of the Geography Department at Miami University (of Ohio).

My research centers on law, citizenship and space. A large part of my existing work has explored this from the standpoint of political activism and protest. More recently, I have turned to examine how states intervene in these dynamics, and so shape the possibilities for citizenship.

I teach courses that generally center on either urban citizenship and politics, or globalization.

In a separate, if connected, life I have been active in various efforts at promoting open technology platforms and standards, from creating the Citation Style Language, to co-creating the Bibliographic Ontology, and contributing to the extensible metadata system in the OpenDocument language.
